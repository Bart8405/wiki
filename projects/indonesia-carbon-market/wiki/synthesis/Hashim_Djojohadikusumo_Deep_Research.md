# Hashim Djojohadikusumo — Deep Research Report

*The president's brother, the country's chief carbon envoy, and a private forestry/mining/water oligarch — a single individual sits at every gate in Indonesia's emerging carbon market. This file maps everything we have on him and tests, name by name, whether his businesses touch the People/Companies/Projects dataset directly.*

---

## 1. Headline finding

**Hashim Djojohadikusumo's personal business empire — Arsari Group and its subsidiaries — does NOT currently appear in any row of `DATASET_people.csv`, `DATASET_companies.csv`, or `DATASET_projects.csv`, and does not appear as a project proponent in any Indonesian Verra PDD we hold.**

That null result is itself the story. As of May 2026, Hashim's forestry concessions in **Bulungan, North Kalimantan** (via PT ITCI Kayan Hutani) and **Penajam Paser Utara, East Kalimantan** (via PT ITCI Kartika Utama) are **announced** as carbon projects but not yet **listed** with Verra or Gold Standard. They sit one step before the dataset — in the regulatory pipeline that Hashim himself is designing.

His leverage on the dataset is therefore **upstream, not lateral**: he doesn't show up as a proponent on any specific VCS#### project, but he is the person writing the rules that govern all 83 projects in `DATASET_projects.csv`.

## 6. The political-economy of why Hashim is the most important name in the dataset

### 6.1 What he is NOT
- He is not a project proponent on any Verra Indonesian project in our dataset (as of May 2026).
- His subsidiaries are not in `DATASET_companies.csv`.
- His employees are not in `DATASET_people.csv`.
- No witness or contact named in our PDDs traces back to Arsari.

### 6.2 What he IS
- The **single official** with both (a) the legal authority to set market rules and (b) hundreds of thousands of hectares of forestry concessions that those rules will commercialise.
- The **public face** of Indonesia's carbon market at COP29 and COP30 — every foreign buyer in the dataset chain (Microsoft, Salesforce, SMBC, etc.) has heard the pitch from him personally.
- The **gatekeeper for international verifiers** — without his sign-off, none of the Verra-side projects could have re-opened in 2025.
- The **family node** — his brother is the President; his nephews include Vice President Gibran Rakabuming Raka (via Prabowo's coalition); his son Aryo runs Arsari's day-to-day and sits on the KADIN Energy & Mineral Resources board.

### 6.3 The trap for foreign buyers / verifiers
For Verra and Gold Standard, signing MRAs with Indonesia means **a state in which the chief climate envoy is also a major future supplier**. Any future revocation, re-issuance dispute, or methodology dispute on his projects will have to be resolved by an agency whose political head is his beneficiary. This is exactly the structural problem the 2023 Rimba Raya license-revocation case exposed — but here it is institutionalised at the level of the rule-setter, not litigated at the level of an individual project.

### 6.4 The trap for civil society
WALHI, Forest Watch Indonesia, AMAN, Mongabay Indonesia, Tempo have all flagged the conflict of interest. **The Ministry of Forestry's Secretary-General Decree No. 31/2024 makes all PBPH licensing processes confidential** ([[Political_Economy_Notes]] §7), which means future Arsari/ITCI permit grants will be processed off-the-record. Hashim's announcement at COP29 came *before* the underlying PBPH paperwork would normally have been visible to outside reviewers.
