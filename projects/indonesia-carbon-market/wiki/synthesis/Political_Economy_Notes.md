## 1. Family conglomerate / dynastic ties

### Widjaja family (Sinar Mas / APP) — VCS4782 + VCS5520 + indirectly via Rio Christiawan

#### a. Direct ownership link to NRS (VCS4782 South Barito Kapuas)
- **PT Nusantara Solusi Raya (NRS)** — the project proponent of VCS4782 — has acquired a new partner: **Megain Widjaja**, grandson of **Eka Tjipta Widjaja**, founder of Sinar Mas Group. Source: Pulitzer Center, "Competition for Kalimantan's Forests" (https://pulitzercenter.org/stories/competition-kalimantans-forests).
- Megain Widjaja is **CEO of Indonesia Clearing House (ICH)**, a subsidiary of the **ICDX Group** (Bursa Komoditi dan Derivatif Indonesia), Indonesia's commodity-futures exchange. ICDX is one of the regulated venues for Indonesian carbon-credit trading. Source: https://www.icdx.co.id/news-detail/corporate-news/megain-widjaja-ceo-indonesia-clearing-house-raih-penghargaan-indonesia-s-top-green-leaders-award-2024.
- In July 2024 Megain won the **Top Green Leaders Award 2024** ("Indonesia Green Leader for Green Orientation in Developing Clean Metal Initiatives") from Warta Ekonomi. ICDX also planted 34,000 mangroves in Bangka Belitung under #GoNeutralCarbon.
- ICDX's own PR sanitises the Widjaja/Sinar Mas family connection — it appears only in the Pulitzer reporting.
- **Why this is politically significant — VERTICAL INTEGRATION:** the same family controls (a) a forest-carbon project (REDD+ supply) and (b) the exchange that prices and clears Indonesian carbon credits (demand-side infrastructure). The "green leader" narrative also gives PR cover.

#### b. Indirect link to PT Suntara Gajapati (VCS5520)
- PT Suntara Gajapati uses an **APP-Group ("Asia Pulp and Paper") progress-reporting tool**. APP is a Sinar Mas subsidiary. So Suntara is operationally tethered to the Widjaja group. Source: https://sustainability-dashboard.com/-/pt.-suntara-gajapati.
- The same dashboard lists **Ata Marie** as Suntara's HCS Assessor (Ata Marie is in DATASET_companies as VCS4782 consultant only — quietly involved in VCS5520 too).

#### c. Rio Christiawan as career bridge from Sinar Mas to forest carbon
- **Dr. Rio Christiawan** is currently CEO of PT Pagatan Usaha Makmur (VCS4967, per 2026 MR) AND Director at **Hutan Synergy** (https://www.hutansynergy.com/about/team). His prior affiliations listed on the Hutan Synergy bio: **Sinar Mas Agro Resources and Technology** (Widjaja palm-oil arm), **Asian Agri** (Sukanto Tanoto / RGE palm-oil arm), **Lippo Plantation** (Riady family), **Orang Tua Group** (Wibisono family).
- He carries direct executive experience inside two of Indonesia's biggest palm/plantation conglomerates (Sinar Mas + Asian Agri/RGE) into the carbon-project ecosystem.

## 4. Bankruptcies, fraud, suspensions

### C-Quest Capital — VCS3327 and VCS3328 (Cookstoves Indonesia 1 & 2) — MAJOR SCANDAL
- **Founder & former CEO Ken Newcombe** charged with fraud by US authorities in **October 2024** for allegedly faking emissions-reduction data on cookstove projects.
- C-Quest Capital filed for **Chapter 7 bankruptcy on 27 February 2025** in Delaware. Sources:
  - https://carbon-pulse.com/373845/
  - https://www.qcintel.com/carbon/article/cookstoves-developer-c-quest-files-for-bankruptcy-37027.html
  - https://verra.org/c-quest-capital-statement/
  - https://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2024-07-03/carbon-developer-reports-former-ceo-to-authorities-over-bad-cookstove-offsets
- **Verra cancelled 5,004,915 VCUs** as overissued across 27 C-Quest cookstove projects. Source: https://verra.org/verra-cancels-5-million-overissued-credits-linked-to-c-quest-capital/.
- Verra halted all 27 CQC cookstove projects. Source: https://www.argusmedia.com/en/news-and-insights/latest-market-news/2582154-carbon-registry-verra-halts-27-cqc-cookstove-projects.
- Washington Post (Sep 2024) reported C-Quest failed to ensure cookstoves were actually being used; Swedwatch reported communities in Zimbabwe got no revenue from the credits sold in their name.
- ICVCM (Integrity Council for the Voluntary Carbon Market) ruled out **most cookstove methodologies** from its CCP-eligible list in March 2025 (https://www.climatechangenews.com/2025/03/07/most-cookstove-carbon-credits-ruled-out-of-quality-scheme-in-push-for-high-integrity/).
- **Implication for the Indonesian projects:** VCS3327 and VCS3328 are part of this universe. Both PDDs have Ken Newcombe as Director. Any future credit issuance is essentially blocked; existing credits face quality-rating discounts and buyer pull-back.

## 5. Dataset hygiene flags / known asymmetries

PDD VCS5524 does mention Sinetics as Technical Advisor (we extracted the contact-person row from there). Either (a) companies CSV is missing the VCS5524 row, or (b) Sinetics's role on VCS5524 was informal. Per standing instruction, companies CSV is not edited; the people CSV reflects what the PDD says. This is the kind of asymmetry that arises when proponents add advisors during operations without revisiting registry filings.
