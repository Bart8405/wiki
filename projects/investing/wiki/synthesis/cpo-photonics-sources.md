## Conferences to watch

- **OFC (Optical Fiber Communication)** — March each year. The big one. Catalysts cluster around this.
- **GTC (NVIDIA)** — Where NVIDIA announces its photonics roadmap.
- **Hot Chips** — Technical deep dives on optical engines.

## How to use these sources

1. **SemiAnalysis + LightCounting** give you the structural view (what's happening over years).
2. **Earnings calls** give you the operational view (what's actually shipping next quarter).
3. **KobeissiLetter + sell-side** give you the sentiment view (what consensus believes).
4. **X feed of execs** (e.g. Jensen, AAOI CEO during earnings season) gives you the leading edge.

If a tweet contradicts an earnings call transcript, the call wins.
