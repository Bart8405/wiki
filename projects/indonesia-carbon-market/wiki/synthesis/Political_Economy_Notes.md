Update this file whenever something interesting surfaces — interlocks between politicians and companies, family conglomerate ties, ex-regulators turning consultant, bankruptcies, opaque ownership, abandoned/suspended projects, anything sketchy.

## 4. Bankruptcies, fraud, suspensions

### C-Quest Capital — VCS3327 and VCS3328 (Cookstoves Indonesia 1 & 2) — MAJOR SCANDAL
- **Founder & former CEO Ken Newcombe** charged with fraud by US authorities in **October 2024** for allegedly faking emissions-reduction data on cookstove projects. ([Bloomberg, July 2024](https://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2024-07-03/carbon-developer-reports-former-ceo-to-authorities-over-bad-cookstove-offsets))
- C-Quest Capital filed for **Chapter 7 bankruptcy on 27 February 2025** in Delaware. ([Carbon Pulse](https://carbon-pulse.com/373845/)) ([QCIntel](https://www.qcintel.com/carbon/article/cookstoves-developer-c-quest-files-for-bankruptcy-37027.html)) ([Verra statement](https://verra.org/c-quest-capital-statement/))
- **Verra cancelled 5,004,915 VCUs** as overissued across 27 C-Quest cookstove projects. ([Verra announcement](https://verra.org/verra-cancels-5-million-overissued-credits-linked-to-c-quest-capital/))
- Verra halted all 27 CQC cookstove projects. ([Argus Media](https://www.argusmedia.com/en/news-and-insights/latest-market-news/2582154-carbon-registry-verra-halts-27-cqc-cookstove-projects))
- Washington Post (Sep 2024) reported C-Quest failed to ensure cookstoves were actually being used; Swedwatch reported communities in Zimbabwe got no revenue from the credits sold in their name.
- ICVCM ruled out **most cookstove methodologies** from its CCP-eligible list in March 2025. ([Climate Change News, March 2025](https://www.climatechangenews.com/2025/03/07/most-cookstove-carbon-credits-ruled-out-of-quality-scheme-in-push-for-high-integrity/))
- **Implication for the Indonesian projects:** VCS3327 and VCS3328 are part of this universe. Both PDDs have Ken Newcombe as Director. Any future credit issuance is essentially blocked; existing credits face quality-rating discounts and buyer pull-back.

## 5. Dataset hygiene flags / known asymmetries

- **Sinetics Accreditation International Taiwan Ltd.** appears in DATASET_people.csv for VCS5524 (via Dr Kai-Hsien Chen) but DATASET_companies.csv only tags Sinetics to VCS4381. PDD VCS5524 does mention Sinetics as Technical Advisor (we extracted the contact-person row from there). ### Hashim Djojohadikusumo — Special Presidential Envoy + carbon market beneficiary

**⚠️ DIRECT CONFLICT OF INTEREST AT THE HIGHEST LEVEL**

- **Hashim Djojohadikusumo** is President Prabowo Subianto's **younger brother** and was appointed **Special Presidential Envoy for Climate and Energy** to COP29 in Baku, Azerbaijan (November 2024). ([Pulitzer Center / Tempo, "New Players in the Carbon Market"](https://pulitzercenter.org/stories/new-players-carbon-market))
- At COP29 (November 11, 2024), Hashim stated that Indonesia has **557 million tons of CO2e** verified and ready for trade and that the government aims to earn up to **US$65 billion** from carbon projects by 2028. ([Pulitzer Center / Tempo, "The Hunt for Carbon Concessions"](https://pulitzercenter.org/stories/hunt-carbon-concessions-indonesias-forests))
- **Environment Minister Hanif Faisol Nurofiq** confirmed that Hashim has been **personally involved in overseeing** the opening of the voluntary carbon market for the forestry sector (Tempo interview, April 16, 2025). ([Pulitzer Center / Tempo, "New Players in the Carbon Market"](https://pulitzercenter.org/stories/new-players-carbon-market))
- Hashim enlisted former **World Bank Managing Director Mari Elka Pangestu** to design the carbon trading mechanism. The MRA with Verra, Gold Standard, Plan Vivo, and ART was being finalized by end of May 2025. ([Pulitzer Center / Tempo, "New Players in the Carbon Market"](https://pulitzercenter.org/stories/new-players-carbon-market))
- **His own family conglomerate is preparing to benefit:** A subsidiary of **Arsari Group** (owned by Hashim) — **ITCI Kartika Utama** — is listed in government documents as a participant in the carbon trading rollout. Through its subsidiary **ITCI Kayan Hutani**, it plans to engage in carbon trading within its forest concession in **Bulungan Regency, North Kalimantan**. ([Pulitzer Center / Tempo, "New Players in the Carbon Market"](https://pulitzercenter.org/stories/new-players-carbon-market))
- Tempo sought comment from Hashim's son **Aryo Puspito Setiaki Djojohadikusumo** and Arsari Group's Public Affairs Head **Nicholay Aprilindo** — neither responded. ([Pulitzer Center / Tempo, "New Players in the Carbon Market"](https://pulitzercenter.org/stories/new-players-carbon-market))
- **Why this is the most politically significant finding in the dataset:** The same person who is (a) drafting Indonesia's voluntary carbon market rules and (b) promoting it internationally to investors is (c) positioned to personally profit through his family's forestry concession. This is not a distant conglomerate relationship — it is the Prabowo family itself.

### Government structure of the carbon market rollout (Prabowo era)

- **Three-wave rollout plan** (per government documents reviewed by Tempo and carbon business sources): ([Pulitzer Center / Tempo, "New Players in the Carbon Market"](https://pulitzercenter.org/stories/new-players-carbon-market))
  - Wave 1 (June 2025): 8 consortiums — Austral Byna/Fairatmos (Muara Teweh), Mayas Project, RER Carbon Project (APRIL/Tanoto, VCS2403), **OKI REDD+ Project (VCS2395)**, **Kubu Peatland Project (VCS5371)**, Seram Climate and Conservation (Sercova), PLUM Peat and Mangrove Conservation and Restoration Project, **South Barito Kapuas Project (NRS/Widjaja, VCS4782)**
  - Wave 2: 17 companies in year 1
  - Wave 3: 35+ companies from year 2
  - Revenue target: **Rp19 trillion (~US$1.1 billion) in year 1**
- **Transparency suppression:** Since May 2024, Ministry of Environment and Forestry **Secretary-General Decree No. 31/2024** classifies ALL PBPH licensing processes as confidential ("to ensure the smooth completion of PBPH applications"). Previously only business work plans were confidential. This made the leaked data Tempo obtained the only public record. ([Pulitzer Center / Tempo, "The Hunt for Carbon Concessions"](https://pulitzercenter.org/stories/hunt-carbon-concessions-indonesias-forests))
- **Minister Raja Juli Antoni** (Forestry) refused to comment on the opaque permit process. When asked about favoritism toward corporations over Indigenous communities at the State Palace (May 28, 2025), he said only: *"Everything's just fine."* ([Pulitzer Center / Tempo, "The Hunt for Carbon Concessions"](https://pulitzercenter.org/stories/hunt-carbon-concessions-indonesias-forests))
- **Riza Suarga** — General Chair of the Indonesia Carbon Trade Association — confirmed government meetings in Sentul City to guide companies through PDD preparation and Verra registration (Tempo interview, April 17, 2025). ([Pulitzer Center / Tempo, "New Players in the Carbon Market"](https://pulitzercenter.org/stories/new-players-carbon-market)) *This is the same Riza Suarga who is founder of Agraus Resources (VCS5475) and former APHI Carbon Team Chair — he is simultaneously the industry association head, a market participant, and a government facilitator.*
