# China (People's Republic of China)

*Great power, rising challenger to the post-Cold War liberal order. Key actor in nearly every contemporary IR debate — world order, regional integration, populism, war and peace.*

## Quick facts

- **Founded (PRC):** 1949.
- **Population:** ~1.4 billion (largest in 2024, recently overtaken by India).
- **GDP:** 2nd largest globally, world's largest exporter of manufactured goods since 2009.
- **Strategic posture trajectory:** Deng's *"bide time, hide brightness"* (1980s) → Hu's *"peaceful rise / peaceful development"* → Xi's *"Chinese Dream / Major Country Diplomacy"* (2010s–).
- **Trilemma corner (Rodrik):** globalization + nation-state — democracy was never on offer.
