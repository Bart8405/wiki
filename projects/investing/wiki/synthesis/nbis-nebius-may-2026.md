*Honest read with bull/bear, valuation math, and what I'd actually do. **NOT financial advice**.*

## What Nebius actually is

A **neocloud** — i.e. an AI-specialized GPU cloud provider, not a general-purpose cloud like AWS. They sell NVIDIA GPU capacity to AI labs and hyperscalers on long-term contracts.

Spun out of Yandex N.V. in 2024 after the Russian operations were sold. Listed on NASDAQ in October 2024. Headquartered in Amsterdam. ([TechCrunch](https://techcrunch.com/2024/10/18/nebius-to-resume-nasdaq-trading-after-severing-ties-with-russia-and-yandex/))

- **Contracted backlog: ~$50 billion** for 2027-2031.
- **Anchored by:** $27B multi-year Meta deal + up to $19.4B Microsoft commitment.
- **2025 full-year revenue:** $530M.
- That's roughly **94x** revenue in contracted backlog. ([Motley Fool](https://www.fool.com/investing/2026/04/19/nebius-has-landed-46-billion-in-ai-cloud-deals-cou/))

### 2. ARR ramp is vertical

## The bear case

### 1. Capital intensity is staggering

- **2026 CapEx ($20-25B) >> 2026 revenue ($3-3.4B).**
- The company is burning massive negative FCF to build capacity for *future* contracted revenue.
- Roughly **~40% of 2026 CapEx still needs to be financed.** That's another ~$8-10B coming. ([Hedge Fund Alpha](https://hedgefundalpha.com/investor-letters/from-russia-with-cash-nebius-group/))

### 2. Dilution is baked in

- $4.6B convertible notes already issued.
- ATM equity program authorized.
- Convertibles convert into equity if the stock keeps ripping. Equity raises will happen if it doesn't.
- Either way, share count is going up.

### 3. Customer concentration

- **Two hyperscalers (Meta, Microsoft) ≈ 90%+ of the backlog.**
- A renegotiation, scope reduction, or technology preference shift by *either* would crush the business model.
- Microsoft is also building its own AI infra; the durability of their NBIS commitment in 2028+ is the open question.

### 4. The neocloud window is temporary

- Today's pricing power exists because NVIDIA GPU supply is scarce.
- When supply normalizes (2027? 2028?), neocloud margins compress hard.
- AWS, Azure, GCP, Oracle will undercut.
- ([Seeking Alpha bear case](https://seekingalpha.com/article/4895073-nebius-the-neocloud-window-is-temporary))

### 5. Valuation is rich

Rough math at current price:
- EV/2026 sales ≈ ~16-18x
- EV/2026 EBITDA ≈ ~40x
- Forward 2027 multiples collapse only *if* they hit the $7-9B ARR target.

If they hit, this is cheap. If they miss by even 20%, the stock has very little support.

### 6. Yandex legacy discount

The Russia/Yandex heritage is legally and operationally severed but institutionally still carries a perception discount.

## What I would actually do

**If I had no exposure and wanted some:**
- **Scale in.** Don't go full size at $220.
- **First tranche** (1/3 of intended size): now, or wait for $210.
- **Second tranche** (1/3): below $200.
- **Third tranche** (1/3): below $180.
- Why: consensus PT is $213.67 — *below* spot. The street isn't yelling buy here. Average it down.

**If I already have exposure:**
- Don't add. Hold.
- Be mentally prepared for a -25-30% drawdown if NVIDIA earnings disappoint or hyperscaler capex commentary cools.

**Why not zero exposure:**
- The 2027-2031 backlog is *contracted*, not pipeline. If they execute even 70% of it, the stock is undervalued at $220.
- NVIDIA Exemplar Cloud GB300 status and $2B equity stake are not minor signals.

**Why not full exposure:**
- The price has run ahead of incremental data points. Each earnings beat now needs to be larger to maintain momentum.
- Dilution is baked in.
- The macro backdrop (Hormuz, yields, NVDA earnings) is hostile to high-multiple stocks.

## The single biggest variable

**Does the 2026 ARR exit hit $7-9B?**

- If yes: stock can re-rate to Citi's $287 or above.
- If they exit at $6B (a "miss"): stock probably drops to $150-170 on the multiple compression.
- If they exit at $5B (a real miss): bear case $125-129 is in play.

You're paying $220 to find out. That's the bet.
