# ASEAN (Association of Southeast Asian Nations)

*Regional bloc of 10 Southeast Asian states. Founded 1967. Defining feature in IR theory: a consensus-based, sovereignty-respecting model of regionalism that contrasts with the supranational EU model.*

## Quick facts

- **Members (10):** Brunei, Cambodia, Indonesia, Laos, Malaysia, Myanmar, Philippines, Singapore, Thailand, Vietnam.
- **Founded:** 1967 (Bangkok Declaration).
- **Decision style:** consensus, non-interference — the "ASEAN Way."
- **Strategic posture (Acharya):** *double-binding* — enmesh both China and the US in regional institutions.
