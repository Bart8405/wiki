Use `==text==` (Obsidian highlight syntax) sparingly and on your own judgment — only for the absolute most crucial terms in a summary, not liberally.

**Why:** The user wants highlights to stand out as genuinely critical. Over-highlighting defeats the purpose.

**How to apply:** In study summaries, pick 2–4 terms per section maximum that are the single most exam-critical concepts and wrap only those in `==...==`. Do not highlight everything that seems important — be selective. When extracting highlights from notes, treat `==...==` as highlights to include alongside other formats.
